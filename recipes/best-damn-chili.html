Best Damn Chili

Description

Chili is a hearty and comforting dish featuring tender chunks of meat, beans, tomatoes, and a medley of spices like chili powder, cumin, and paprika. It's simmered slowly to develop complex flavors and a rich, thick texture, making it a perfect warming meal for chilly days. The spicy kick from the chili peppers adds an extra dimension of flavor that keeps you coming back for more.


Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 Anaheim chile pepper, chopped
  • 2 red jalapeno pepper, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle light beer
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 (12 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle pepper sauce
  • 2 ½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (16 ounce) cans dark red kidney beans
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3 tablespoons chopped resh cilantro
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat; cook and stir onion, bell pepper, Anaheim pepper, jalapeno peppers, and garlic in the hot oil until softened.

  3. Step 3

    4. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Add Worcestershire sauce and garlic powder. Crumble bouillon cubes over beef and add beer. Continue to cook , scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet, until liquid is hot, about 3 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Stir beef mixture into pepper mixture.

  6. Step 6

    7. Stir crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and wine to the beef mixture. Season with chili powder, 2 tablespoons cumin, brown sugar, pepper sauce, basil, paprika, salt, oregano, and black pepper.

  7. Step 7

    8. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and simmer until meat and vegetables are very tender and flavors have developed in the chili, about 90 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  8. Step 8

    9. Mix kidney beans into beef and vegetables. Continue to simmer until beans are hot, about 30 minutes more.

  9. Step 9

    10. Blend sour cream, cilantro, and remaining 1/2 teaspoon cumin in a food processor until smooth. Serve sour cream mixture with chili.
